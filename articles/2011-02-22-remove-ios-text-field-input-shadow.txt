--- 
title: Remove iOS text field input shadow
date:  22/02/2011
tag:   iOS iPhone PhoneGap

By default, the iOS WebView will add a top shadow to `<input />` elements.

    input[type="text"] {
        -webkit-appearance:none;
    }

You may want to apply a custom border, such as:

    input[type="text"] {
        border:1px solid black;
    }
